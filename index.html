IMAGinAI


Art Bidding & Building Webapp


The purpose of the website is to provide a platform for digital art enthusiasts to buy, sell, and create unique pieces of digital art. The website aims to create a community of artists and art lovers, and democratize the process of buying and selling digital art.
By providing a user-friendly interface for both art listers and bidders, the website makes it easier for people to discover and bid on unique pieces of digital art.

AI POWERED NUTRITION ANALYZER


For Fitness Enthusiasts

Food is vital to human life and has long been a focus of healthcare conferences. This project aims to build a model for fruit classification based on characteristics such as color, shape, and texture. Users can capture images of different fruits and send them to the trained model, which analyzes the images to detect nutritional content such as sugar, fiber, protein, and calories. Nutrients play a significant role in human metabolism and can impact health. The AI-powered Nutrition Analyzer assists individuals seeking proper nutrition to achieve fitness, manage illnesses, or lead a healthy lifestyle. With Artificial Intelligence, a reliable nutrition analyzer was developed capable of displaying nutritional content when provided with an image of the food.
